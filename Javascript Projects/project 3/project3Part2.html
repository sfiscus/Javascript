Problem Statement

Write a program that will prompt a user for a student's name and the student's exam score out of 100. Your program will match the exam score to a letter grade and print the student's name, score, and grade to the page. Calculate the letter grade from this table. Remember that exam scores may have decimal portions, and are not just whole numbers.

Exam ScoreAssigned Grade
90 and aboveA
80 to less than 90B
70 to less than 80C
60 to less than 70D
below 60 F
